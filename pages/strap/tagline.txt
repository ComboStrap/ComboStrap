====== Strap Template - Tagline ======


===== About =====
The [[strap:strap|Strap template]] supports the [[doku>config:tagline|tagline]] [[:configuration|configuration]].

It's a short and appealing description of your website or product.

It's used:
  * in the ''description'' field of the [[strap:manifest|manifest]]
  * in the home page [[semantic:title|title]].
