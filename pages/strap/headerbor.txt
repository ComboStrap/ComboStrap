====== Strap Template - Headerbar ======


===== About =====
The [[strap|strap template]] supports the creation of a page called  ''headerbar'' that will be rendered at the top of the template.


The ''headerbar'' is supposed to contain a ''navbar''. See [[getting_started:5_headerbor|Creation of a headerbar]].

===== Articles Related =====
{{backlinks>.}}

===== Features =====
  * The ''headerbar'' follows the same convention than  a [[doku>faq:sidebar|sidebar]]
  * If there is a ''headerbar'' for a namespace, this ''headerbar'' in place of the root ''headerbar'' will be rendered.
