====== Strap Template - Sidekick Slot ======

===== About =====
The ''sidekick slot'' is a [[slot|slot]] that is shown at the right side of the content.


===== Articles Related =====
{{backlinks>.}}

===== How it works =====
This is just an extension of the standard [[slot|slot feature]] of [[doku>|Dokuwiki]]

  * Create a page called ''sidekickslot'' 
  * And you will see its content appears at the right side of the page.

===== Caching =====

The cache of the side slots is automatically deleted when a page in the same namespace is created, added or deleted.
See [[:sideslots]]

===== Configuration =====
==== Name ====

You can configure the name of the ''sidekickslot'' with the ''tpl»strap»sidekickslot'' [[:configuration|configuration]]
