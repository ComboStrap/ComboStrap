====== Strap - Automatic Side Slot Content Management ======


===== About =====
The ''side slots'' (ie [[strap:side_slot|side]] and [[strap:sidekick_slot|sidekick]] slots) content are always up-to-date and does not require to disable the cache, increasing [[:performance|performance]].


<note>
If you have any [[https://www.dokuwiki.org/caching|%%~~NOCACHE~~%%]] instructions in your side slots, just delete them.
</note>

===== Articles Related =====
{{backlinks>.}}
===== Why ? =====

''side slots'' contains generally [[ui:navigation|navigational component]] such as a [[ui:ntoc|list of page]]. Therefore, their content becomes generally stale when a page is modified, added or deleted. 

The basic solution is to disable cache but this action has an important impact on [[:performance|performance]] because they read the index.

===== Implementation =====


The cache:
  * is scoped to the namespace (directory) of the requested page
  * is automatically deleted when a page of this namespace is modified, added or deleted
