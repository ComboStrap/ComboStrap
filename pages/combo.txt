====== Combo ======




<card>
''combo'' is the powerhouse of the ''ComboStrap''.

It's a [[doku>plugins|Dokuwiki plugin]] that complements the [[strap:strap|Strap template]].

It contains most of the UI and functional components such as:
  * the [[ui:ui|UI Components]]
  * the [[url:url|Url Manager]]
  * the [[semantic:semantic|Meta Manager]]
  * the [[css|CSS optimizer]]
  * the [[quality:quality|quality module]]
  * the [[analytics:analytics|analytics module]]

This [[ui:card|UI card]] is one of the many UI examples.
