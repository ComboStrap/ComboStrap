====== Combo plugin ======




<card>
The ''combo'' plugin is a combo of [[doku>plugins|Dokuwiki plugins]] that complements the [[strap:strap|Strap template]].

It contains most of the components such as:
  * the [[ui:ui|UI Components]]
  * the [[url:url|Url Manager]]
  * the [[semantic:semantic|Meta Manager]]
  * the [[css|CSS optimizer]]

This [[ui:card|UI card]] is one of the many examples.
