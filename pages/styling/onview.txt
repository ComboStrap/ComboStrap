====== ComboStrap Styling - OnView ======


===== About =====
''OnView'' is an attribute that permits to set an animation to a [[ui:block|block component]] when they are visible or enter the screen.

===== Example =====

==== Animation 1 ====

<webcode name="OnView" frameborder="0">
<code dw>
<title  onView="fadeInLeft">A title with a fadeInLeft animation</title>
</code>
</webcode>
