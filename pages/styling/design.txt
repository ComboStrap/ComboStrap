====== ComboStrap Styling - Design System ======


===== About =====
The ''ComboStrap design'' system takes all the good part of [[strap:Bootstrap|Boostrap]] but does not stop there as it also follow the [[https://material.io/|Google Material Design recommandations]]


===== The plus =====
With ''ComboStrap'', you get the possibility:
  * to apply a [[skin|skin]] to your component in order to set the level of emphasis.
  * to [[elevation|elevate]] a single component.
