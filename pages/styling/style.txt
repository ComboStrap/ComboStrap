====== ComboStrap Styling - HTML Style attribute ======


===== About =====
''ComboStrap'' supports the low level styling of its [[docs:docs|component]] via pure CSS:
  * in a [[#attribute|attribute]]
  * or in a [[#style|style component]]



<note tip>
The style attribute and component are low level access to [[styling:styling|style your components]]. If you just need to change a property, check the [[styling:styling|high level attribute]].
</note>

===== Type =====

==== Attribute ====

Because every [[docs:docs|ComboStrap UI component]] are a [[styling:html|superset of HTML]], the ''CSS'' ''style'' attribute is also supported. 
example with a [[docs:block:card|card]] 
<webcode name="Default" frameborder=0 width=100% scrolling=yes externalResources="," renderingMode=story >
<code dw>
<card 
    style="
        color:white;
        background-color:purple;
        margin: 0 auto;
        width:50%;
        text-align: center;
        font-size:1.2rem
        "
>
A centered card with a centered white text against a purple background 
</card>
</code>
</webcode>
