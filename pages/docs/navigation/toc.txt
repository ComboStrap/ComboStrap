====== ComboStrap UI - Table of Content (TOC) ======

===== About =====
The ''toc (table of content)'' is actually a feature of the [[docs:utility:renderer|combo renderer]]. 

It will be actually printed below the first [[docs:navigation:outline|outline heading]] ang gives to the reader an overview of your document.
