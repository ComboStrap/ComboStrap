====== ComboStrap SEO - Quality Content ======

===== About =====
The [[seo:seo|SEO quality module]] aims to measure and keep the quality of the website high.

This module regroups the following functionalities:
  * [[strap:hidden]]
  * [[:low_quality]]

===== Configuration =====
==== low quality page not public ====
To keep low quality page private and not made them available to search engine, you can enable this feature with the [[:configuration]] ''lowQualityPageNotPublicEnable''.
