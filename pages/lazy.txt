====== ComboStrap - Lazy Loading ======


===== About =====
''lazy loading'' is the loading of resources such as image only when they are needed.

''ComboStrap'' supports by default the lazy loading of:
  * [[ui:raster|raster image (png, jpg, ...)]]
  * [[ui:svg|svg]]
