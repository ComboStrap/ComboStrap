====== ComboStrap UI - Highlighting ======


===== About =====
To highlight a sequence of words in ComboStrap, you needs to wrap the words with two quote characters ''%%''%%''.

''Highlighting'' permits to add [[styling:emphasis|emphasis]] on a couple of words.
