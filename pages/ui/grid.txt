===== Horizontal Centering =====

To center a box horizontally, use the class ''mx-auto''. 

Example with a [[card|card]] and some [[color|colors]]
<webcode name="Default" frameborder=0 width=100% scrolling=yes externalResources="," renderingMode=story >
<code dw>
<card class="mx-auto" width="300px" background-color="purple" color="white">
A centered card with a purple background 
</card>
</code>
</webcode>
