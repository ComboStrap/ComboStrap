====== ComboStrap UI - Dropdown ======

===== About =====
''dropdown'' is a [[ui|component]] that shows a ''dropdown menu''. 

It must be placed inside a [[group|group]] element of the [[navbar|navbar]] that you will find at the top of your page in a [[strap:headerbor|headerbar]]

This component accepts as entry a series of link. If the linked page does not exist, it will be rendered in red.
