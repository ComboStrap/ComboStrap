====== ComboStrap UI - Brand ======

===== About =====
''brand'' is a [[ui|component]] that goes generally in the left side of the [[ui:menubar|menubar]]. 

It highlights and helps styling the ''brand'' depicted as text and/or [[ui:icon|icon]] and adds a [[link|link]] to your home page.
