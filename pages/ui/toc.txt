====== ComboStrap UI - Table of Content (TOC) ======

===== About =====
The ''toc (table of content)'' is actually a feature of the [[renderer|combo renderer]]. 

It will be actually printed below the first heading with the the outline created by the [[heading|headings]] ang gives to the reader an overview of your document.
