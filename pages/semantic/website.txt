====== ComboStrap Metadata - Website ======


===== About =====
The ''home'' page contains ''website'' [[semantic:semantic|metadata]] that are parsed by search engine such as [[semantic:google|google]].

They contains information about your website and your organization.


===== Articles Related =====
{{backlinks>.}}

===== Usage =====
This information is used by Google and others search engine.
  * The logo is shown in [[https://developers.google.com/search/docs/data-types/logo|the Google knowledge panel]]
  * The website information adds a [[https://developers.google.com/search/docs/data-types/sitelinks-searchbox|searchbox]].
===== List =====
The following elements are added to the home page:
  * ''url''
  * ''name'', the [[doku>config:title|title]]
  * ''description'', the [[semantic:tagline|tagline]]
  * ''image'', the [[getting_started:4_favicon|logo]] (as png)
