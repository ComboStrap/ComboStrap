====== ComboStrap Metadata - Article Page ======


===== About =====
''article'' is a [[type|type]] of page.


An article, such as a news article or piece of investigative report. Newspapers and magazines have articles of many different types and this is intended to cover them all.

===== Property =====

https://developers.google.com/search/docs/data-types/article#article-types

Only a marked-up image that directly belongs to the article should be specified. Images should be at least 696 pixels wide.

^ Key ^ Description ^
| ''published_time'' | When the article was first published. |
| ''modified_time'' | When the article was last changed. |
| ''expiration_time'' | When the article is out of date after |
| ''author'' | Writers of the article |
| ''category'' | A high-level section name. E.g. Technology |
| ''tag'' | Tag words associated with this article. |
