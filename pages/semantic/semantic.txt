====== ComboStrap - Page Metadata (Semantic) ======


===== About =====
The ''metadata manager'' is composed of the [[frontmatter|frontmatter module]] that permits to set the following metadata
      * [[canonical|canonical]]
      * [[description|description]]

and enables what we called a ''semantic component'' such as:
  * [[url:redirection|URL redirection processing]]
  * [[automatic_canonical|Automatic Canonical]]
  * [[url:permalink|Permalink]]
